27Description
28 Standard high Reynolds-number k-omega turbulence model for
29 incompressible and compressible flows.
30
31 References:
32 \verbatim
33 Original model:
34 Wilcox, D. C. (1998).
35 Turbulence modeling for CFD
36 (Vol. 2, pp. 103-217). La Canada, CA: DCW industries.
37
38 Two-phase adaptation:
39 Amoudry, L. O. (2014).
40 Extension of k-omega turbulence closure to two-phase sediment
41 transport modelling: Application to oscillatory sheet flows.
42 Advances in Water Ressources, 72, 110-121
43 \endverbatim
44
45 The default model coefficients are
46 \verbatim
47 twophasekOmegaCoeffs
48 {
49 Cmu 0.09;
50 alphaOmega 0.52;
51 betaOmega 0.072;
52 C3om 0.35;
53 C4om 1.0;
54 KE2 1.0;
55 KE4 1.0;
56 alphaKomega 0.5;
57 alphaOmegaOmega 0.5;
58 Clim 0.0;
59 sigmad 0.0
60 popeCorrection false;
61 }
62 \endverbatim
63
64 For the revisited version of the model from Wilcox (2006),
65 model coefficients should be modified following:
66 \verbatim
67 twophasekOmegaCoeffs
68 {
69 betaOmega 0.0708;
70 alphaKomega 0.6;
71 Clim 0.875;
72 sigmad 0.125
73 popeCorrection True (only for 3D cases);
74 }
75 \endverbatim
76
77 This file is in the \dir turbulenceModels/RAS/twophasekOmega subdirectory
78 inside the \dir turbulenceModels/RAS subdirectory
79 inside the \dir turbulenceModels subdirectory
